02What it does

Deploy, transit, submerge, and surface — without ever changing platforms.

Tempest penetrates A2/AD-denied zones by air, enters the water under control, runs submerged ISR to depth, then surfaces to relay over RF. One operator, one asset, no mid-mission handoff between an aerial drone and an undersea vehicle that were never built to talk to each other.
